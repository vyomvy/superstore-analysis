🛒 Superstore Sales Analysis
Data-Driven Business Insights by Sant (Vyom)

📌 Objective

Analyze supermarket sales data to uncover profitability gaps and identify high-performing markets.

🧠 Business Problem

High sales do not always translate into high profit. This project highlights cities that generate revenue but reduce overall business value.

📊 Key Insights

Loss-Making Cities: Philadelphia, Houston, Chicago

→ High sales but negative profitability

Top Profit Cities: New York City, San Francisco

→ Strong balance of revenue and profit

Growth Potential: Springfield, Los Angeles

→ Opportunity for expansion
