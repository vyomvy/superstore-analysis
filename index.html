🛒 Superstore Sales Analysis

📌 Objective

Analyze supermarket sales data to identify profitability gaps and uncover high-performing markets.

🧠 Business Problem

High sales do not always mean high profit. This project identifies cities that generate revenue but reduce overall business value.

📊 Key Insights

Loss Cities: Philadelphia, Houston, Chicago

Top Profit Cities: New York City, San Francisco

Growth Potential: Springfield, Los Angeles
